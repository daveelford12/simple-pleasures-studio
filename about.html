About

Simple Pleasures Studio is a small collection of illustrated moments — reminders to slow down, take care, and keep going in ordinary ways.

The work here isn’t about fixing anything. It’s about noticing what’s already being carried: tired mornings, half-finished coffees, small comforts, modest triumphs. The kind of moments that don’t look like much from the outside, but quietly matter.

Each print is designed to feel calm and familiar. A gentle pause. A soft acknowledgement that doing what you can is often enough. There’s humour here, but it’s light and kind. Nothing loud. Nothing forced.

The illustrations are created intentionally and kept simple. Choices are limited on purpose — a small number of prints, a consistent format, a few carefully considered sizes. Everything is printed to order and made to last, without excess or urgency.

This studio isn’t chasing trends or big statements. It’s interested in the everyday — in reassurance rather than motivation, in steadiness rather than noise.

If something here makes a day feel a little lighter, that’s enough.
